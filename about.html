MaisonTub story

A More Organized Start for Walk-In Tub Planning

MaisonTub was created for homeowners who want a calm, structured way to begin a walk-in tub request. Instead of guessing which service category fits, repeating the same details, or confusing platform matching with direct installation, MaisonTub keeps the process focused: submit project information, compare available provider options where coverage exists, and decide whether to continue after reviewing provider terms.

MaisonTub Is a Matching Platform, Not a Direct Walk-In Tub Provider

MaisonTub does not install, sell, repair, inspect, manufacture, diagnose, or provide medical advice. The platform helps homeowners submit request details and compare available responses from participating providers where available.

Platform role Request organization and provider matching support. Provider role Final pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service terms. Homeowner role Review available responses and choose whether to continue.
