2. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files or pieces of information stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website remember certain actions, preferences, or technical details.

Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, scripts, or browser-based identifiers.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

MaisonTub may use several categories of cookies or similar technologies depending on website configuration.

Essential cookies These support core website functionality, security, cookie banner behavior, navigation, and basic request form operation.
Preference cookies These may remember choices such as cookie consent status or interface preferences.
Analytics cookies These may help understand general website usage, page performance, traffic patterns, and user interaction trends.
Marketing or referral cookies These may help understand how visitors reached the website or support advertising measurement where applicable.

MaisonTub may use browser local storage to remember cookie banner choices and support a smoother website experience.
