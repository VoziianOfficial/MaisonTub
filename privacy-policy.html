MaisonTub is an independent provider-matching platform. Final terms come from participating providers.

1. Overview

MaisonTub helps homeowners submit walk-in tub project details for provider-matching consideration where participating providers are available.

MaisonTub does not install, replace, convert, repair, inspect, sell, manufacture, or directly provide walk-in tub services. MaisonTub does not provide medical, health, mobility, or safety advice.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily submit through forms, calls, email links, or other website interactions.

  • Contact information, such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Project information, such as selected service category, bathroom layout notes, access preferences, timing, and message details.
  • Technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and interaction data.
  • Consent and communication records related to submitted requests.

3. How We May Use Information

Information may be used to operate the website, process requests, support provider-matching functions, respond to inquiries, improve user experience, and maintain platform security.

  • To receive and review submitted walk-in tub project details.
  • To help route requests to participating providers where available.
  • To communicate about submitted requests or platform-related questions.
  • To monitor website performance, prevent misuse, and improve functionality.
  • To comply with applicable legal, operational, or security requirements.

4. Information Sharing

MaisonTub may share submitted request information with participating providers or operational service providers when reasonably necessary to support provider-matching, communication, security, hosting, analytics, or form processing.

Participating providers are independent businesses. Their services, pricing, warranties, licensing, insurance, schedules, and agreements are not controlled by MaisonTub.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

MaisonTub may use cookies or similar technologies to remember cookie choices, support website functionality, understand general usage, and improve site performance.

You can adjust cookie choices through your browser settings. Some features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

For more detail, review the Cookie Policy.

6. Your Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have choices regarding access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain personal information.

  • You may choose not to submit a request form.
  • You may contact MaisonTub to ask about information associated with your request.
  • You may use browser controls to limit cookies.
  • You may choose whether to continue with any participating provider response.

7. Security and Retention

MaisonTub uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect submitted information. However, no website, internet transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Information may be retained as reasonably necessary for request handling, communication, recordkeeping, legal compliance, security, and operational purposes.

8. Children’s Privacy

MaisonTub is intended for use by adults seeking information about provider-matching options. The website is not directed to children.

9. Updates to This Policy

MaisonTub may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the effective date shown on this page.
